About Us

We are a collection of students who are passionate about cyber security. We are currently studying at Amikom University Yogyakarta, Indonesia. This project was created for our serious attention regarding the number of wiretapping and data leaks that are very detrimental to many parties. Our concept of making this web page like being in the world of cyberpunk is because we hope that apart from making it easier for users, another hope is that it can attract the younger generation to be more aware of the security of personal data. We hope that this project can provide information regarding real cyber threats to all people and this tool can be used to protect the important data that you have.-Alt5chm3rz
